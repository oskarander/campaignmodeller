Calculation Methodology

1. Blended Price: Weighted average price based on the Single vs. Return split. Price = (SingleRate × S_Price) + (ReturnRate × R_Price × DiscountMultiplier)

2. Transaction Model: Shares are now applied to the Total Transaction count, not Revenue. Trans = Total_Transactions × (Share% / 100) Revenue = Trans × Price

3. PAX (Boardings): PAX = Trans × Size × (1 + ReturnRate) (Note: ReturnRate 100% means 2 trips per transaction)

Campaign Logic

The "Return Discount" campaign is applied exclusively to the return portion of Challenger pricing. The "Flex Upsell" is calculated as a transaction-level premium.
